Events

Acoustic Fridays

 

Every Friday night from 10pm-1am Drip shows off the local music talent free to our customers.

Book Club

 

Twice a month the local Greensburg Book Club meets to discuss their latest selection.
They're always looking for new members.

Annual Dripper 5k

 

Every year on October 27th the Drip Coffee Bar sponsors a 5k run with the majority of
the proceeds benefiting the local Food Bank. There is an entrance fee, but you'll get a cool t-shirt
build a table with price info.
